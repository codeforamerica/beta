Update: Thanks to everyone who attended BETA in person or watched our livestream online. It was great to share the stories of our work this year and get feedback from the Code for America community. Check back for full recordings of the event in the near future.

BETA brought together Code for America friends and family to tell stories of work that has been done so far this year. Fellows and staff discussed stories around civic engagement, what it's like to work in City Hall, and other key insights into the civic tech movement.

Thanks for joining us to demo projects, talk about opportunities and challenges and give the fellows feedback to help inform the year going forward. We hope you also enjoyed the chance to network with the CfA community.
