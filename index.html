A civic show and tell by Code for America
June 19th, 2014 4:00 - 9:00 PM
155 9th St, San Francisco

be·ta /ˈbātə/ Advice on how to successfully complete a particular route or path (climbing).


BETA will bring together Code for America friends and family to tell stories of work that has been done so far this year. Fellows and the wider CfA community will discuss stories around civic engagement, what it's like to work in City Hall, and other key insights into the civic tech movement.

Join us as we demo projects, talk about opportunities and challenges and give the fellows feedback to help inform the year going forward. Following the presentations will be a reception, and a chance to network with the CfA community.
