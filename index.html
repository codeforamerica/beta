Update: Thanks to everyone who attended BETA in person or watched our livestream online. It was great to share the stories of our work this year and get feedback from the Code for America community.

BETA brings together Code for America friends and family to tell stories of work that has been done so far this year. Fellows and staff will discuss stories around civic engagement, operational discovery, what it's like to work in City Hall, and other key insights into the civic tech movement.

Please do join us for this years event to talk about opportunities, challenges, and CFA announcements. As always it will be a great opportunity to network with the CFA family.
