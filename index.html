BETA is an intimate gathering for Code for America and our friends to tell stories of the work we've done this year.


Fellows and the wider CfA community will discuss stories around civic engagement, what it's like to work in City Hall, and other key insights into the civic tech movement.

Join us as we demo projects, talk about opportunities and challenges and give the fellows feedback to help inform the year going forward.

Following the presentations will be a reception, and a chance to network with the CfA community.
